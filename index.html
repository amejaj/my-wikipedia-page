Ameja Johnson

Ameja Johnson is a 3rd year Cybersecurity major at Penn State Berks working as a part-time sales associate at Wawa inc.

Early Life

Johnson grew up in Philadelphia PA, born and raised. As a small child, Johnson attended Francis D Pastorius Elementry Charter School. Graduating in 8th grade with High Honors in all academic Studies. Throughout those years, Johnson played sports like basketball, flag football as well as participating in drill team and dance. She then graduated from Philadelphia E&T Charter High School, making the deans list every term. She worked fast food jobs throughout high school to gain some work experience. Johnson started her own business during this time doing hair and makeup. It was a hobby that started at a young age that she became so good at she had to pursue it.

Career

Johnson initially went into college studying psychology but was introduced to IST her second semester. Something about it grasped her attention and she never looked back. She fully committed to the major and now loves every second of it. She now has experience with python and java, and gain a numerous amount of certificates in needed courses. It was not easy but she managed to get this far all while working part-time for Wawa, running her small businesses, and being the President of PSU Berks Black Student Union. You can look up her business brand name on Instagram under mbstylzz. She opens bookings when she is home on breaks for school. Johnson have hopes to maybe on day start her own Cybersecurity company one day after college keeping the same brand name MBSTYLEZZ, working to protect businesses from cyber-attacks and bugs.
